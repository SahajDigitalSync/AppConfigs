Introduction & Scope

Axial Labs ("we", "us", or "our") operates the mobile applications CONNECT-ƎD, DoctorDocs, and others (collectively, the "Services"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Compliance Note: This policy is designed to align with global standards including the GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation), CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act), and the Information Technology Act (India).

Types of Data Collected

Personal Information

We do not require you to provide personal identifiers (like your real name or physical address) to use basic game features. However, if you create an account, register for a tournament, or contact support, we may collect:

  • Account Info: Username, email address, and profile picture.
  • Support Interactions: Name and email provided during support tickets.
  • Social Login: If you sign in via Google or Apple, we receive an anonymized identifier and associated email.

Non-Personal & Usage Data

We automatically collect technical data to improve app performance and experience:

  • Device IDs: Advertising IDs (IDFA/AAID), device model, and OS version.
  • Gameplay Stats: Level progress, achievements, and session duration.
  • Network Info: IP address (anonymized) and general location (Country/City level).

How We Use Your Data

We process your information for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To save your progress, sync data across devices, and manage leaderboards.
  • Personalization: To tailor in-game offers and adjust difficulty based on performance.
  • Monetization: To deliver relevant advertisements and manage in-app purchases.
  • Security: To prevent fraud, cheating, and unauthorized access to our servers.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share information with trusted partners who provide essential services:

  • Advertising: Google AdMob, Unity Ads, and AppLovin for delivering and measuring ads.
  • Analytics: Firebase (Google) and Meta Pixel to understand how the app is used.
  • Cloud Infrastructure: Google Cloud and Supabase for secure data storage and authentication.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Our games are intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If we discover a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately from our servers.

For users in the EU, the minimum age is 16 unless local law provides for a lower age. We comply with GDPR-K requirements.

Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access/Portability: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Deletion: Request that we erase your personal data ("Right to be Forgotten").
  • Opt-Out: You can reset your Advertising ID in your device settings or opt-out of personalized ads within the app menu.

Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption (AES-256) and secure protocols (HTTPS) to protect your data during transfer and at rest. While no system is 100% secure, we regularly audit our partners' security practices.
